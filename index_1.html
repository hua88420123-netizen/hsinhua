Situation
Product launches required tight handoffs across hardware engineering, firmware, manufacturing, channel operations, and marketing. The average cycle was running long — creating revenue commitment risk. Late-arriving spec sheets, ambiguous ownership at each gate, and slow cross-team readiness signals were the main friction points.
Action
Mapped the full process end-to-end and identified three root friction points. Redesigned the handoff model with a single shared status dashboard, clear ownership definitions per gate, and structured pre-briefs to surface blockers earlier in the cycle. Drove adoption across five functions simultaneously.
Result
Average cycle time shrank by 30%. Quality improved — fewer last-minute scope cuts at launch. The redesigned model was adopted as the company default for all subsequent product launches, signaling full stakeholder buy-in.
